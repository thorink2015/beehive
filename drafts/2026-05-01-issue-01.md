Welcome aboard, and thanks for being here. This is the first issue, so here is the short version of what to expect.

## What this is

A weekly note on **the one thing worth your attention** in the field this week. No fluff, no twenty-minute reads, no jargon for its own sake.

## Who it's for

- Operators who run a sprayer and want practical, current info
- Agronomists tracking rule changes and product updates
- Anyone who'd rather get the gist in two minutes flat

## The schedule

Once a week, same day, always skimmable on a phone in a field. If a section ever runs long, that's a mistake on our end, not yours.

> Good spraying is mostly timing and attention. This newsletter is here to help with both.
